# ☕ Starbucks Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Starbucks sales performance, customer purchasing patterns, and product insights. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations, enabling business users to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Starbucks sales using Power BI. It includes multiple dashboard pages that allow users to explore sales performance across different dimensions such as products, time, and nutritional information.

The dashboard focuses on:

- Sales Performance Analysis
- Product Performance
- Customer Purchase Trends
- Hourly Sales Analysis
- Daily Sales Analysis
- Nutritional Composition of Menu Items

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Daily Sales Dashboard
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Sales Amount
- Total Quantity Sold
- Orders by Hour
- Sales Amount by Hour
- Quantity Sold by Hour

---

### 2. Weekly Sales Dashboard
- Orders by Day of Week
- Revenue by Day of Week
- Quantity Sold by Day of Week
- Hour-wise Product Filtering
- Product Price Overview

---

### 3. Item Analysis Dashboard
- Product-wise Nutritional Analysis
- Calories per Item
- Product Categories
- Carbohydrates, Protein, Fat, and Fiber Distribution
- Interactive Product Selection
